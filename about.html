About me

I am Blend Melenica, a 19-year old from Vushtrri. My father Nexhmedin is from Vushtrri as well, while my mother Fatmire is from Vuthaj, Montenegro. I have two sisters , they are both older than me and both have finished medical school. I grew up in Vushtrri, only recently having come to Prishtina for studies alongside my siblings. I went to a public middle school and to Mehmet Akif College for High School. When the opportunity arose for me to enroll in RIT-K I just could not turn it down!

I am a man of many interests and hobbies, web development and programming being one of them. I have enrolled in many courses to further my knowledge on these topics and i aspire to get a job this summer in the field I love!
